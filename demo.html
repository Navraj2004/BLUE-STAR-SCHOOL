Blue Star School Singrijan (BSSS), located in Dimapur, Nagaland, is a co-educational school established in 2003. It is managed by the Pvt. Unaided and serves students from grades 1 to 8. The school is situated in the Singrijan area of the Dhansiripar block in Dimapur district. It has 300+ students and 20+ teachers with English as the medium of instruction.

The school has 10 classrooms, 2 non-teaching rooms, and is approachable by an all-weather road. It has private building with barbed wire fencing, electricity, tap water, 1 boys toilet, 1 girls toilet, a playground, 5 functional computers, ramps for disabled, and no library or computer-aided learning lab. Recently, the school felicitated HSLC pass out students during a program held on June 5, 2025. Academic session starts in April.

Vision

To provide wholesome education in a conducive environment, to all classes of society, that nurtures values, develops social and leadership skills, enhances experiential learning, and leads to the development of 21st century skills. The school offers opportunities to discover oneself and contribute to society as confident, smart individuals.

Objectives

  1. To provide a safe, secure, and conducive environment where children can enjoy learning, achieve excellence, and become lifelong learners.
  2. To provide programs supporting all-round development of the child.
  3. To inculcate values in children and enable them to look beyond "me, mine & myself."
  4. To develop children into self-directed learners.
  5. To imbibe in children the qualities of self-discipline, self-confidence, and leadership.
  6. To create awareness and appreciation in children about their rich culture and tradition, while being open to other cultures of the world.
  7. To provide learning opportunities and activities that cater to different learning styles.
  8. To foster internationalism through a global dimension in the curriculum and interaction with global organizations and institutions.
  9. To provide state-of-the-art facilities, infrastructure, and technology to enhance teaching and learning outcomes.
